Frequently Asked Questions

What is Settlr?

Settlr is an expense-splitting app that helps you track shared costs with friends, family, or housemates — and settle up balances simply.

How do I join a group?

Ask the group creator to share the invite code or link. Tap "Join Group" in the app and enter the code, or tap the invite link directly.

How does splitting work?

When adding an expense, you can split it equally among group members, by exact amounts, or by percentage. The app automatically updates everyone's balances.

How do I settle up?

Go to the group and tap "Settle Up". The app will show you the optimal payments to resolve all balances. Record a settlement once payment is made outside the app.

Can I use Settlr with people who don't have the app?

Yes — you can add guest members to a group. Guests receive a link to view the group without needing to register.

I found a bug. What should I do?

Please email us with as much detail as possible (device, OS version, steps to reproduce) at support@settlr.tech. We appreciate it!
