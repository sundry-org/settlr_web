Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation

Words with capitalised initial letters have meanings defined below. These definitions apply whether the words appear in singular or plural form.

  • Application – refers to Settlr, the mobile app provided by the Company.
  • Company ("we", "us", "our") – refers to Settlr.
  • Device – any device that can access the Service (computer, phone, tablet).
  • Personal Data – any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual.

  • Usage Data – data collected automatically during use of the Service.
  • You – the individual or entity accessing the Service.

Usage Data is collected automatically and may include your device's IP address, browser type, app version, pages or screens visited, time and date of visits, time spent, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

  • With your consent – for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

We retain your Personal Data only as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
